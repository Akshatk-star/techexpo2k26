ABOUT TECH EXPO

Where innovation meets inspiration. Discover the minds behind the mission.

About TECH EXPO 2K26

TECH EXPO 2K26 is the annual technical extravaganza organized by the students of HMR Institute of Technology and Management, Delhi. Now in its 10th edition, it has grown into one of Delhi-NCR's most prestigious inter-college technical fests.

This year, we bring together 2000+ participants from 50+ colleges across India for 25+ events spanning coding competitions, robotics, hackathons, research, gaming, and hands-on workshops — all under one roof.

Our mission is simple: to ignite the engineering spirit, foster collaboration between the brightest young minds, and create a launchpad for tomorrow's innovators.

🎯 VISION

To build a platform where technical excellence, creativity, and innovation converge to shape the engineers of tomorrow.

🚀 MISSION

To provide every participant an equal opportunity to showcase talent, learn from industry leaders, and grow through challenge.

// THE INSTITUTE

HMR INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

HMR Institute of Technology and Management, Delhi, is an AICTE-approved institution affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. Located in Hamidpur, Delhi, the institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering and Management.

With state-of-the-art labs, highly qualified faculty, and a vibrant student community, HMR consistently ranks among top technical institutes in Delhi.
